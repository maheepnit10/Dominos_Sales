🍕 Domino’s Pizza Sales Analysis (MySQL Project)
________________________________________
🧩 Project Overview
This project analyzes Domino’s Pizza sales data using MySQL to uncover valuable insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and revenue trends.
The goal is to derive data-driven strategies to boost sales and forecast demand for the year 2025.
The project simulates a real-world business analytics scenario—combining data cleaning, querying, aggregation, and interpretation into meaningful business outcomes.
________________________________________
🎯 Objectives
✔️ Identify sales trends and customer preferences.
✔️ Determine top-selling pizzas by revenue and quantity.
✔️ Evaluate sales distribution by size, category, and time of day.
✔️ Forecast potential sales growth for 2025 using historical data.
✔️ Provide actionable recommendations to improve business decisions.

📈 Business Insights Summary
🔹 Medium & large pizzas dominate total revenue share.
🔹 Weekend evenings record the highest number of orders.
🔹 Veg pizzas outperform in Tier-2 cities; non-veg pizzas dominate metros.
🔹 Combo offers and discounted bundles during off-peak hours could boost sales by ~10–12%.
🔹 Forecasting suggests 11% growth in annual sales for 2025 under current trends.
